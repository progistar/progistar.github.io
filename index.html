About Us

The Computational Biology Lab at Kookmin University focuses on developing innovative computational approaches to uncover novel biological insights from multi-omics data. We integrate genomics, transcriptomics, and proteomics to explore tumor antigens, immune responses, and therapeutic targets.

Research Topics

  • Proteogenomics and Neoantigen Discovery
  • Multi-omics Data Integration for Cancer Immunology
  • Machine Learning for Biological Sequence Analysis
  • Development of Open-source Bioinformatics Tools
